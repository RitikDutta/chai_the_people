How it Works

  1. Visit your nearest Indian tea stall
  2. Open the Chai ThePeople web app and scan the QR code
  3. Fill out the survey that pops up on your screen
  4. Get a cup of tea for free!

Unique Features

  • Survey questions are not repeated, providing a fresh and exciting experience each time you visit.
  • Earn rewards and climb up the leaderboard by filling out surveys regularly.

Benefits

  • Get a free cup of tea
  • Help tea sellers earn profits
  • Provide valuable feedback to the government for improving economic development policies

The Impact of Chai ThePeople

Chai ThePeople is more than just a free tea app. It is a tool for economic development that helps local tea sellers earn more profits, while also providing valuable feedback to the government to improve policies and create more jobs. By using Chai ThePeople, you are supporting the local economy and helping to build a better future for India.
